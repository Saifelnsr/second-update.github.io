Green Monitoring Network

Green Monitor Project aims to measure the gass emissions that are emitted by illegal factories and the temperature of the environment.
These gasses are represented in:
1. VOC (Volatile Organic Compounds) Such as (Benzene, Toluene, Formaldehyde and Alcohol).
2. CO2 .
3. Temperature.
This project given for the government to detect the illegal factories & Decide either to demolish the factory or suggest filtration systems:

Technical Reprot

Mechanism:


1. VOC (MS1100) Sensor: This sensor is from PID’s (Photoionization detectors). Which receives the ionization energy that is required to ionize the gas molecules. This sensor is made from Metal oxide semiconductor (MOS) surface. MS1100 is typically made from SnO2. When VOC gases come to contact with the oxygen adsorbed on the surface of the sensor it increases sensor conductivity which is proportional to voc concentration. Sensor is more sensitive at feverish temperatures as the resistance increases significantly. This sensor required a preheat of 5 minutes to ensure the precision of the measurements.
2. MQ-135: MQ sensors series typically have the same mechanism as the MS1100 detecting mechanism. MQ sensor is made from the same materials (SnO2) which receive gas molecules and give them space to react with the oxygen on the surface of the MOS (Metal oxide semiconductor).
3. DHT22: This sensor measures both humidity and temperature. It measures humidity by capacitive sensor that works on the capacitor. It measures the temperature through resistor with a mechanism called (NTC) Negative Temperature Coefficient). The thermistor works by increasing the temperature. Then, the resistance decreases. The sensor interprets the change in resistance to change in temperature.

Sensor’s integration:


Some relations were made between the parameters of the project. The relation between temperature & VOC concentration increases substantially when the temperature increases to double. This relationship ensures the integration between these two parameters in detecting illegal factories.
